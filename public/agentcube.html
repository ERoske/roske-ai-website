Objections, Answered

The questions every CFO asks before buying.

Will it hallucinate on our financial data?
AgentCube does not generate financial figures. It retrieves them, via official Oracle APIs, and hands the structured data back to your AI. The AI explains what the numbers mean; it does not invent them. Every query is a real call to your Essbase, Planning, or FCC system, and every response is auditable.
How long does it take to deploy?
Weeks, not quarters. AgentCube connects to your Oracle systems using the authentication your team already runs (Basic, OAuth, OIDC/SSO). There is no schema migration, no data warehouse, and no rip-and-replace. Most of the time is your security review, not the implementation.
Does it work with our existing Oracle security model?
Yes. AgentCube respects your existing provisioning. If a user can see an entity in Planning, the agent can see it on their behalf. If they can't, it can't. Role-based access, row-level security, and identity federation all carry through.
Which AI models does it support?
Anything that speaks the Model Context Protocol. Claude, ChatGPT, Microsoft Copilot, Gemini, and your in-house agents all plug in using the same interface. When your standard changes, AgentCube does not need to.
Is our data sent to OpenAI, Anthropic, or Google?
That is entirely your choice. AgentCube is the connector, not the model. Deploy it on-premises and point it at your own private AI. Deploy it in your cloud and use your enterprise AI contract. The data leaves your boundary only if you configure it to.
What does pricing look like?
AgentCube is sold by Caprus.AI as a licensed engine with enterprise deployment support. Pricing scales by the number of Oracle providers connected and the scope of rollout. We walk through it on the demo call, when we know which systems you actually need.
What's the catch?
The catch is that it only works as well as the data in your Oracle systems. AgentCube cannot fix a broken close process or a forecasting model your team does not trust. What it will do is stop hiding those problems behind three-day Smart View digs.
