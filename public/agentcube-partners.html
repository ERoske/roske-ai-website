Partner Questions, Answered

The terms every consulting firm asks before signing.

Is the program exclusive in my region?
No exclusivity at Certified or Premier tier. Elite partners can negotiate regional or vertical preferences, but we do not grant blanket exclusivity before a partner has proven commercial traction. We'd rather have multiple great partners than one partner with exclusive rights and no delivery.
Can we white-label AgentCube as our own product?
No. AgentCube is Caprus.AI's product. You can co-brand solution offerings built on AgentCube ("Your Firm Smart Close, powered by AgentCube"), but the underlying product stays Caprus-branded. This protects your clients with a supported, versioned platform instead of a one-off custom build your firm has to maintain alone.
Who sells the license, us or Caprus?
You can sell directly under a reseller agreement, or refer Caprus to sell and earn the same margin as a referral fee. Both structures are supported. Most partners prefer the reseller model to control the relationship; some prefer the referral model to keep procurement simpler. Elite partners often use both depending on the deal.
What happens if a client we brought in churns?
Your renewal annuity stops when the client's subscription ends. There are no clawbacks on first-year margin. If churn is tied to a product issue, we fix the product. If churn is tied to a services issue, that's between you and your client.
What's the certification commitment?
Roughly 3 days of intensive technical training plus a practical assessment per consultant. We cover Oracle provider integration, deployment, security configuration, and common client customizations. We run cohorts quarterly. Some self-paced content is always available.
How does Caprus compete with partners on services?
We don't. Caprus does not sell EPM implementation services. We run a small professional services team for product support, escalations, and co-delivery with partners who request it, not as a standalone services business. If a client insists on buying EPM services directly from Caprus, we route them to a tiered partner.
Can we build custom tools on top of AgentCube for specific clients?
Yes. AgentCube is designed to be extended. Certified partners build client-specific tools regularly; Premier partners can commission new provider support from Caprus; Elite partners get priority on the custom provider roadmap. Your IP in what you build stays with you.
What about data security liability?
Caprus is responsible for the security of the AgentCube platform itself (code, updates, authentication mechanisms). Partners are responsible for the implementation at the client (deployment configuration, role-based access, client data handling). Standard partner agreement language covers the split in detail.
