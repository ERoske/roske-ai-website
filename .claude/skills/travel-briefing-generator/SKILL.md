# Travel Briefing Generator

Generate comprehensive travel briefings for business trips. Time to create: 30-45 minutes.

## Purpose

Transform travel chaos into organized preparation. Consolidates itinerary, meeting prep, local information, and logistics into a single reference document.

**Be extremely concise. Sacrifice grammar for the sake of concision.**

## Input Requirements

1. **Trip Details**: Destination, dates, purpose
2. **Travel Logistics**: Flights, hotel, ground transportation
3. **Meeting Schedule**: All meetings/events with attendees and purpose
4. **Personal Preferences** (optional): Dietary restrictions, exercise preferences, personal commitments

## Core Structure (Always Include)

### Trip Overview
- Destination, dates, purpose
- Key objectives and success criteria

### Quick Reference Card
One-page essentials:
- Flight times and confirmations
- Hotel name, address, phone
- Emergency contacts
- Time zone, weather, currency

### Flight & Transportation
- Outbound/return flight details
- Ground transportation (airport to hotel, getting around)
- Check-in tips and travel times

### Accommodation
- Hotel details, check-in/out times, confirmation number
- Amenities (gym, Wi-Fi, restaurants)
- Proximity to meetings

### Meeting Schedule
For each meeting:
- Date, time, location (with travel time from hotel)
- Meeting type, attendees, purpose
- Prep status and materials needed

### Meeting Preparation Summary
- High-priority meetings requiring advance prep
- Total prep time needed
- When to complete (before trip or on plane)

### Local Intel
- Weather and what to wear
- Time zone management
- Currency, payments, tipping
- Language and cultural norms (if international)

### Packing List (Location-Specific Items Only)
**Only include items unique to this trip**:
- Weather-specific (warm coat for cold destinations, rain gear for rainy seasons)
- Activity-specific (hiking shoes/knee brace for Edward if outdoor activities, swimsuit if pool/beach)
- Special equipment (camera for scenic trips, binoculars for wildlife)
- Location-specific (adapters for international, altitude medication for high elevation)

**Do NOT include standard items** (toiletries, underwear, socks, phone charger, laptop, etc.)

### Post-Trip Follow-Up
- Within 24 hours: thank yous, business development, admin
- Within 1 week: follow-up with leads, content creation

## Optional Sections (Add If Needed)

### Dining Recommendations
- By location (near hotel, near meetings)
- By meal type

### Downtime & Wellness
- Exercise options
- Things to do if free time

### Work Schedule & Availability
- When you'll be reachable
- Time zone coordination with team

## Usage Notes

**Time to Create**: 30-45 minutes

**When to Use**: Business trips, speaking engagements, client visits, conferences

**Research**: Weather forecasts, restaurant recommendations, hotel amenities, transportation options

**Voice**: See CLAUDE.md for Edward's voice and tone guidelines.

**Packing List Philosophy**: Only list items unique to this specific trip. Examples:
- Cold destination → warm coat, gloves, hat, ice scraper
- Beach trip → swimsuit, sunscreen, sunglasses
- Hiking trip → hiking shoes, knee brace for Edward, day pack, water bottles
- International → power adapters, currency, translation app
- High altitude → hydration tablets, altitude medication
- Business event → business cards, presentation materials

Assume standard items are always packed (toiletries, underwear, phone charger, etc.)
